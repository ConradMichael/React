User Story:
As a Training Manager, I want to view the Trainer Skills so I can find the appropriate trainers.

Acceptance Criteria:

Given I am on the Trainer Skills page,
When I click the dashboard button
Then I am taken to the dashboard view

Given I am on the Trainer Skills page,
When I click the Trainer button
Then I am taken to the trainer view

Given I am on the Trainer page,
When I click the Skills button
Then I am taken to the trainer skills view

Given I am on the Trainer Skills page,
When I click the back button,
Then I am taken to the trainer view
